Babice is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Krzywcza, within Przemyśl County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres west of Krzywcza, 22 km west of Przemyśl, and 41 km south-east of the regional capital Rzeszów.
The village has a population of 892.
